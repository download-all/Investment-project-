About Us

Bit-Digital Mining harnesses the powerful synergies between high-performance computing (HPC), Bitcoin mining, and Ethereum staking, optimizing computational resources for maximum efficiency. As a pioneer in the HPC/AI space, Bit Digital spans across the United States, Canada, and Iceland with a focus on providing carbon-free, future-ready compute power.
